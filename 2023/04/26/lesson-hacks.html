Rules for binary operations(0.45):
For a difference of two binary numbers, 0-0 is 0, 0-1 is 1(carried over), 1-0 is 1, and 1-1 is 0.
For a product of two binary numbers, 0x0 is 0, 0x1 is 0, 1x0 is 0, and 1x1 is 1.
For a quotient of two binary numbers 0/1 is 0, and 1/1 is 1.
